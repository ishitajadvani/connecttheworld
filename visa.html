Visa Assistance

Hassle-free visa support for 50+ destinations. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on the journey.

Home › Visa Assistance
Simple Process

How It Works

Our streamlined visa process makes international travel stress-free. From document collection to visa delivery, we handle everything.

1
Choose Destination
Tell us where you want to go and we'll identify the exact visa requirements, processing times, and fees for your nationality.
2
Submit Documents
Our team provides a tailored document checklist. You submit your documents — passport, photos, bank statements, and more — to our office or online portal.
3
We Process
Our experienced visa team reviews your application, liaises with embassies, and tracks the status. We keep you updated every step of the way.
4
Receive Your Visa
Once approved, your visa is delivered to your doorstep or emailed to you if it's an e-visa. You're ready to travel!
For UAE Residents

Visa Requirements by Country

Visa requirements for UAE passport holders and UAE residents. Requirements may vary — contact us for the latest information.

🇬🇧
Visa Required
United Kingdom
Standard Visitor Visa required. Processing time: 3–8 weeks. Valid for up to 6 months.
🇺🇸
Visa Required
United States
B1/B2 Tourist Visa required. Interview at embassy. Processing time: 2–8 weeks.
🇹🇷
e-Visa
Turkey
Easy online e-Visa available. Multiple entry, 90 days stay. Processing: 24–48 hours.
🇹🇭
Visa on Arrival
Thailand
Visa on arrival for UAE passport holders. 30-day stay. AED 110 fee at airport.
🇯🇵
Visa Required
Japan
Tourist visa required. Processing: 5–7 business days. Single or multiple entry options.
🇲🇻
Visa Free
Maldives
No visa required for all nationalities. Free 30-day tourist stamp on arrival.
🇲🇾
Visa Free
Malaysia
Visa-free entry for UAE passport holders. 30-day stay. No prior arrangement needed.
🇮🇩
Visa on Arrival
Indonesia
Visa on arrival at major airports including Bali (DPS). 30 days, extendable once.
🇫🇷
Schengen Required
France
Schengen visa covers France and 26 other EU countries. Valid up to 90 days in 180.
🇨🇭
Schengen Required
Switzerland
Schengen visa required. Apply via Swiss embassy in UAE. Processing: 2–4 weeks.
🇦🇺
ETA / Visa Required
Australia
Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) or visitor visa required. Apply online or through us.
🇸🇬
Visa Free
Singapore
Visa-free for UAE passport holders. 30-day social visit pass issued on arrival.
Document Checklist

Standard Required Documents

While requirements vary by country, most visa applications require the following documents. Our team will provide a tailored list for your specific destination.

📘
Valid Passport
Minimum 6 months validity beyond your travel dates. At least 2 blank pages.
📸
Passport-Sized Photos
Recent white background photos meeting the embassy's specifications (usually 35x45mm).
🏦
Bank Statement
Last 3–6 months of bank statements showing sufficient funds for your trip.
🛡️
Travel Insurance
Comprehensive travel insurance covering medical expenses (€30,000 minimum for Schengen).
🏨
Hotel Confirmation
Proof of accommodation bookings for all nights of your stay, with cancellation policy.
✈️
Travel Itinerary
Detailed day-by-day travel plan or confirmed flight tickets (round-trip) with dates.
